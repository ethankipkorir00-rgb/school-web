Academics at Greenvale School

At Greenvale School, we provide a rigorous and inspiring academic program that prepares students for success in secondary school and beyond.

We focus on student growth through strong core subjects, enrichment electives, and a learning environment that supports confidence and curiosity.

What We Offer

Our curriculum includes English, mathematics, science, social studies, arts, music, foreign languages, and technology.

Each student receives personalized support from experienced teachers who encourage critical thinking, effective communication, and leadership.
