Admissions at Greenvale School

Welcome to Greenvale School Admissions. We are excited to welcome new learners into our vibrant community and support every family through the application process.

Our admissions team is committed to making enrollment simple, transparent, and friendly. We accept applications from students who are ready for a positive, challenging, and supportive learning experience.

Application Process

To apply, please complete the admissions form, submit academic records, and provide a short personal statement. Interviews and assessment activities are arranged as needed to understand each student’s strengths and learning goals.

We welcome applicants from all backgrounds and look for students who show curiosity, resilience, and a desire to grow.

Key Admissions Dates

  • Open application period: January 15 - March 31
  • Assessment window: April 10 - April 24
  • Offer letters sent: May 15
  • Registration deadline: June 15

If you need accommodations or have special enrollment requests, please contact our admissions office early so we can support your needs.

What We Look For

  • Academic curiosity: Students who enjoy learning and ask thoughtful questions.
  • Community spirit: Learners who contribute positively to school life.
  • Strong habits: Students who show resilience, responsibility, and respect.

Our admissions team reviews each application carefully and considers the whole student, including academic readiness, character, and potential.

Contact Admissions

For questions or help with your application, please reach out to our admissions team at admissions@greenvaleschool.edu.

We look forward to meeting your family and sharing more about life at Greenvale School.
